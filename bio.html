Bio

Jerald Macachor

Software Developer

Navy Veteran, Scientist, MBA

Jerald Macachor was born in November 11, 1987 in Cebu City, Philippines. His family moved to United States in 2004 in Las Vegas, Nevada. He attended high school at Palo Verde and graduated in 2007. After attending college at University of Nevada Las Vegas (UNLV) for a year, he enlisted in the U.S. Navy in 2008 and served as a Hospital Corpsman.

Jerald served for 6 years in the Navy and was stationed primarily in San Diego California. He worked at the Naval Medical Center San Diego in Balboa Park assisting in Surgeries as a Surgical Technologist. For 6 months in 2012, Jerald went on deployment in the Middle East and Africa. He spent brief periods in Bahrain, the UAE, Oman, Djibouti, and the Seychelles. Jerald finished active duty in 2013, and went into the reserves.

Jerald used his GI Bill to pursue a Biology degree at University of California San Diego, graduating in 2014. Subsequently earned an MBA and Masters in Biotechnology at Johns Hopkins University. He learned programming skills from his Bioinformatics courses at Johns Hopkins and used those skill to enter the Software industry.

Jerald currently resides in Irvine, California with his wife Francesca "Franchy" and baby daughter Ada. He's taking a Full Stack Immersive Course at San Diego Global Knowledge University. He enjoys coding, playing guitar, practicing jiu jitsu, and Salsa and Batchata dancing with his wife.
